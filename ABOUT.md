# 👋 Hey, I'm StreamStack

> **AI-Native Screen Recording & Voiceover Studio**
> Born from frustration. Built for creators.

---

## 🎬 Who Am I?

I'm the open-source lovechild of **Screen Studio** and **VibeVoice**, raised by a community of developers who were tired of:

- Paying $29/month just to add smooth zooms to screen recordings
- Spending hours writing voiceover scripts
- Recording the same demo 10 times because "umm... let me try again"
- Waiting for AI tools to generate 2-minute audio clips

So I was built to be **different**.

---

## ✨ What Makes Me Special?

### 🧠 AI-First, Not AI-Bolted-On

Most screen recorders were built for the 2010s, then someone duct-taped an "AI feature" to them last week. I was designed from day one with AI in my DNA:

## 🚀 My Origin Story

**Week of April 2026:** 

A developer was looking for interesting GitHub projects to build something new. They found:

1. **openscreen** — Great screen recording, but no AI
2. **VibeVoice** — Amazing voice AI, but just a model
3. **deer-flow** — Powerful agents, but for research

The idea hit: **What if we combine them?**

48 hours later, I was born. Not as a commercial product. Not as a startup pitch. But as a **tool for creators**, by creators.
